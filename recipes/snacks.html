Description

This is a popular African snack known as puffpuff. It is eaten mostly in west Africa.

Ingredients:

  • flour
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Juice

Steps:

  1. Mix the floor with salt
  2. Cut the pepper
  3. Mix the pepper and flour
  4. Fry the flour in the vegetable oil
  5. Serve and eat
