Continental

Description

This is a popular Continental meal known as rice steak. It is eaten mostly in the Caribbean.

Ingredients:

  • Steak
  • Vegetables
  • Pepper
  • Cheese
  • Juice

Steps:

  1. Roast the steak
  2. Cut the Vegetables
  3. Cut the pepper
  4. Mix the vegetables and pepper
  5. Serve and eat
