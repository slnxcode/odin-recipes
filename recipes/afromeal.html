Afromeal

Description

This is a popular African meal known as fufu. It is eaten mostly in west Africa.

Ingredients:

  • Yam flour
  • Vegetables
  • Pepper
  • Meat
  • Juice

Steps:

  1. Pound the yam flour
  2. Cut the Vegetables
  3. Cut the pepper
  4. Mix the vegetables and pepper
  5. Serve and eat
